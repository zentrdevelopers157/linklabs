Privacy Policy

Short version: we collect almost nothing, share nothing with anyone by default, and you can ask us to forget you at any time. Here's the long version.

1. Who we are

LinkLabs is a four-person web studio based in India. Our contact email is linklabs.studio@gmail.com. This policy applies to linklabs-three.vercel.app and any subdomain we control.

2. What we collect

  • Form data — when you fill the contact form, we receive whatever you choose to send (name, email, project details, phone, budget). We use this only to reply to you.
  • Newsletter — if you subscribe, we store your email to send monthly notes. Unsubscribe link is in every email.
  • Cookies — we set one localStorage value to remember your cookie banner choice. No third-party cookies, no tracking pixels, no ad networks.
  • Server logs — Vercel (our hosting provider) keeps standard access logs (IP, user-agent, request path) for ~30 days for security and debugging. We don't read them unless something breaks.

3. What we do not collect

  • No Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other third-party tracker
  • No fingerprinting, no session recording, no heatmaps
  • No data brokers, no ad networks, no resale of any kind

4. How long we keep your data

Form inquiries: kept in our email inbox for as long as we're working together, then archived or deleted. Newsletter: until you unsubscribe. Logs: ~30 days (Vercel's default).

5. Your rights

You can ask us at any time to:

  • Tell you what we have on you
  • Correct or delete it
  • Stop sending you anything

Email linklabs.studio@gmail.com and we'll action it within 7 days.

6. Third parties we use

  • Vercel — hosting and edge delivery
  • Web3Forms (optional) — backup form delivery, only if you submit the form
  • Cal.com (optional) — if you book a call, their privacy policy applies
  • Google Fonts — fonts are loaded from Google's CDN; Google sees the request

None of these services sell your data. We picked them for that reason.

7. Children's privacy

Our site is not directed at children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect data from them.

8. Changes to this policy

If we change anything material, we'll bump the date at the top and post a note on our homepage. Continued use of the site after a change means you accept the new policy.

9. Contact

Questions, complaints, or data requests: linklabs.studio@gmail.com
